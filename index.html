Key Shorcuts:
  • 1, 2, 3, 4 to cycle through preset camera positions
  • p = play/pause animation
  • o = speed up animation
  • i = slow down animation
  • x = negative translation on x-axis. X = postive translation on x-axis
  • y = negative translation on y-axis. Y = postive translation on y-axis
  • z = negative translation on x-axis. Z = postive translation on z-axis
  • a = postive rotation on x-axis. A = negative rotation on x-axis
  • b = positive rotation on y-axis. B = negative rotation on y-axis
  • c = positive rotation on z-axis. C = negative rotation on z-axis
  • s = postive rotation of view on x-axis. S = negative rotation of view on x-axis
  • d = positive rotation of view on y-axis. D = negative rotation of view on y-axis
  • f = positive rotation of view on z-axis. F = negative rotation of view on z-axis
  • w = negative translation of view on x-axis. W = postive translation of view on x-axis
  • e = negative translation of view on y-axis. E = postive translation of view on y-axis
  • r = negative translation of view on x-axis. R = postive translation of view on z-axis
  • After making a selection, please click anywhere on the screen to deselect the input.
    This allows the keyboard shortcuts to work better.
